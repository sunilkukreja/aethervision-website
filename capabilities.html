Our Capabilities: Engineering Mission Advantage

Core Competencies

AetherVision LLC transforms advanced scientific concepts into practical, mission-enhancing solutions. We possess deep expertise and proven experience in developing and applying disruptive technologies tailored for the unique demands of the Intelligence Community (IC) and Department of Defense (DoD).

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML)

  • Developing and deploying sophisticated AI/ML algorithms for real-time multi-INT data analysis, automated threat detection, complex pattern recognition, predictive modeling, and enhanced situational awareness.
  • Expertise in optimizing AI/ML models (including deep learning, computer vision, NLP) for deployment on resource-constrained edge devices (SoCs, FPGAs) and embedded systems crucial for tactical ISR and autonomous platforms.
  • Proven experience in building secure, scalable AI/ML platforms (cloud and edge) for processing sensitive mission data (e.g., experience architecting on AWS GovCloud, Azure Government).
  • Application areas include automated target recognition (ATR), behavioral analysis, anomaly detection, sensor fusion, and decision support systems.

Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance (ISR) Solutions

  • Designing, integrating, and enhancing advanced ISR sensor payloads and processing systems for diverse platforms (including UAVs, UGVs, and distributed networks).
  • Expertise in integrating and fusing data from multi-modal sensor suites (EO/IR, radar, SIGINT, acoustic, seismic) for comprehensive, real-time situational awareness.
  • Implementing robust on-platform edge computing solutions for rapid, localized data processing, reducing latency and bandwidth requirements in contested environments.
  • Developing sophisticated autonomous navigation, path planning, and multi-platform coordination algorithms enabling operation in complex, GPS-denied, or communication-limited scenarios.
  • Engineering secure, resilient communication capabilities (including concepts for LPI/LPD optical communications) for reliable data transmission in contested domains.

Neuromorphic Vision & Advanced Sensing

  • Leveraging deep expertise in neuromorphic (event-driven) visual perception, inspired by biological systems, for next-generation sensing capabilities.
  • **Proven DARPA Success:** Successfully led and executed the DARPA FENCE program, delivering a TRL-6 neuromorphic camera prototype that exceeded performance milestones, demonstrating enhanced sensitivity and reduced cognitive load.
  • Expertise in designing custom Readout Integrated Circuits (ROICs) and Focal Plane Arrays (FPAs) for specialized, high-performance neuromorphic and multi-spectral imaging applications (UV-THz concepts).
  • Developing event-based vision systems offering significant advantages: ultra-high temporal resolution, wide dynamic range, low power consumption (low-SWaP), and superior performance in detecting/tracking fast, low-observable targets in cluttered or low-light conditions.
  • Research extends to neuromorphic tactile sensing and other bio-inspired sensor modalities for enhanced robotic perception and interaction.

Data Analysis, Exploitation & Fusion

  • Creating novel data analysis methodologies and software tools to extract timely, actionable intelligence from large-scale, complex, multi-source data streams (Multi-INT: SIGINT, GEOINT, MASINT, HUMINT, OSINT).
  • Expertise in developing and applying advanced predictive analytics, anomaly detection algorithms, and pattern recognition techniques to identify subtle indicators and emerging threats.
  • Designing and implementing automated intelligence analysis workflows to accelerate the intelligence cycle and enhance analyst decision-making.
  • Proficiency in secure data management and establishing robust data governance frameworks for sensitive mission data, ensuring integrity, accuracy, and compliance (experience exceeding NASA standards).

Expert Consulting, Training & Strategic Planning

  • Providing expert technical consulting services in AI/ML applications, ISR system design, data analysis strategies, neuromorphic vision integration, and multi-INT fusion techniques.
  • Offering independent technology assessments, system architecture design, integration support, and strategic technology roadmapping aligned with specific agency missions and program goals.
  • Developing and delivering customized training programs on advanced technology concepts and applications for government and industry partners.
  • Leveraging extensive experience in navigating the government R&D ecosystem (including SBIR/STTR) to provide strategic guidance.

Technology Focus Areas & Solutions

Building upon our core competencies, AetherVision concentrates on developing and integrating innovative solutions to address critical national security capability gaps:

  • Next-Generation Sensing & Perception Solutions: Engineering advanced sensor systems exploiting neuromorphic principles, multi-modal fusion (EO/IR, RF, acoustic, seismic, etc.), and novel materials (e.g., plasmonics concepts) for superior detection, tracking, and identification (DT&I) in challenging environments (low-light, high-clutter, high-speed, GPS-denied). Applications include persistent surveillance, enhanced ISR, platform survivability, and force protection.
  • AI-Powered Multi-INT Exploitation & Decision Support: Creating sophisticated AI/ML algorithms optimized for real-time analysis of diverse intelligence data streams (Multi-INT). Focus includes automated pattern recognition, anomaly detection, predictive threat modeling, and intuitive decision support tools deployable from secure cloud environments (e.g., GovCloud) to tactical edge devices for information advantage.
  • Enhanced Autonomous Systems Capabilities: Integrating advanced perception (including neuromorphic vision), AI-driven control algorithms, secure communications, and resilient navigation techniques into unmanned platforms (UAVs, UGVs, Swarms) to improve mission effectiveness, operational reach, adaptability in complex terrains, and survivability in contested or denied environments.
  • Advanced Defensive Technology Concepts: Researching and conceptualizing novel materials (e.g., graphene metamaterials, BNNTs), advanced sensor concepts, and AI-driven countermeasures to protect critical assets against emerging threats, including Directed Energy Weapons (DEW) and sophisticated electronic warfare (EW) tactics.
  • Custom Hardware Acceleration & Low-SWaP Solutions: Designing and leveraging specialized hardware, including custom ROICs, FPAs, Field-Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGAs), and System-on-a-Chip (SoC) solutions, to accelerate AI/ML algorithms and sensor processing for demanding low-Size, Weight, and Power (low-SWaP) applications on embedded systems and edge platforms.
