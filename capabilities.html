Our Capabilities

Core Competencies

AetherVision LLC specializes in the research, development, and application of disruptive technologies tailored for the unique demands of the Intelligence Community (IC) and Department of Defense (DoD).

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML)

  • Developing advanced AI/ML algorithms for real-time data analysis, threat detection, pattern recognition, and predictive modeling.
  • Optimizing algorithms for resource-constrained edge devices and embedded systems critical for field deployment.
  • Expertise in deep learning, machine learning, natural language processing (NLP), and computer vision applications.

Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance (ISR) Platforms

  • Designing and developing advanced ISR platforms, including unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) and unmanned ground vehicles (UGVs).
  • Integrating multi-sensor systems (EO/IR, radar, SIGINT) for comprehensive situational awareness.
  • Implementing real-time data processing on edge devices (SoCs) for rapid decision-making.
  • Developing autonomous navigation algorithms and secure communication capabilities for contested environments.

Neuromorphic Vision Systems

  • Leveraging deep expertise in neuromorphic (event-driven) visual perception for next-generation sensing.
  • Designing custom Readout Integrated Circuits (ROICs) and Focal Plane Arrays (FPAs) for specialized high-performance applications.
  • Developing systems with high temporal resolution, high dynamic range, and low power consumption for detecting and tracking fast, low-observable objects.

Data Analysis and Exploitation

  • Creating novel data analysis methods and tools to extract actionable intelligence from complex, multi-source data streams.
  • Expertise in multi-INT fusion (SIGINT, GEOINT, MASINT, HUMINT, OSINT).
  • Developing advanced predictive analytics and anomaly detection algorithms.
  • Automating intelligence analysis workflows and enhancing decision-making through intuitive data visualization.

Consulting and Training

  • Providing expert consulting services in AI/ML, ISR, data analysis, neuromorphic vision, and multi-INT integration.
  • Offering technology assessment, system design & integration support, and strategic planning guidance.
  • Developing and delivering tailored training programs for government and private sector clients.

Technology Focus Areas & Solutions

Building upon our core competencies, AetherVision is focused on developing and integrating innovative solutions to address critical national security needs:

  • Next-Generation Sensing & Perception: Developing advanced sensor systems leveraging neuromorphic principles and multi-modal fusion (EO/IR, RF, acoustic, etc.) for superior detection, tracking, and identification in challenging environments, including low-light, high-clutter, and high-speed scenarios. Applications include enhanced ISR, platform protection, and situational awareness.
  • AI-Powered Intelligence Exploitation: Creating sophisticated AI/ML algorithms for real-time analysis of diverse intelligence data streams (Multi-INT). Focus includes automated pattern recognition, anomaly detection, predictive modeling, and decision support tools deployed both in cloud environments and directly on edge devices for tactical advantage.
  • Autonomous Systems Enhancement: Integrating advanced perception, AI-driven control, and secure communication technologies into unmanned platforms (UAVs, UGVs) to improve mission effectiveness, operational range, navigation in complex terrains, and resilience in contested communication environments.
  • Advanced Defensive Technologies: Researching and developing novel materials, sensor concepts, and AI-driven countermeasures to protect critical assets against emerging threats, including directed energy weapons (DEW) and sophisticated electronic warfare tactics.
  • Custom Hardware Acceleration: Designing and leveraging specialized hardware, including custom ROICs, FPAs, FPGAs, and SoCs, to optimize AI/ML algorithms and sensor processing for low-SWaP (Size, Weight, and Power) applications on embedded systems and edge platforms.

AetherVision is committed to pushing the boundaries of technology to provide decisive advantages for our clients.
