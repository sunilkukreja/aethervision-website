Government Contracting & Partnering

SDVOSB Certified Partner

AetherVision LLC is a verified Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), committed to serving the mission needs of the U.S. Government. This status allows government agencies to engage with us through set-aside contracts and sole-source opportunities, facilitating rapid access to our innovative technologies and specialized expertise.

We are registered in the System for Award Management (SAM.gov) and actively pursue opportunities across the DoD, Intelligence Community, and other federal agencies.

Commitment to Compliance & Security

We understand the critical importance of security and compliance in national security R&D. AetherVision LLC is committed to implementing robust cybersecurity practices aligned with relevant standards such as NIST guidelines and DFARS 252.204-7012 requirements. We are prepared to meet Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) requirements as mandated by specific contracts.

Our operations maintain awareness of export control regulations (ITAR/EAR) relevant to defense technologies, ensuring proper handling of sensitive information and intellectual property throughout the development lifecycle.

Contracting Vehicles & Focus

AetherVision LLC leverages its SDVOSB status and technical strengths to pursue various contracting mechanisms:

  • SBIR/STTR Programs: Actively seeking Phase I, Phase II, and Phase III opportunities across DoD components (DARPA, AFRL, ARL, etc.) and Intelligence Community agencies (IARPA, NRO, etc.) aligned with our core competencies.
  • Set-Aside Contracts: Targeting SDVOSB set-aside solicitations appropriate for our capabilities.
  • Sole-Source Opportunities: Leveraging unique qualifications for potential sole-source awards under the SDVOSB program.
  • Subcontracting & Teaming: Partnering with prime contractors and other small businesses on larger efforts.
  • Broad Agency Announcements (BAAs): Responding to relevant BAAs seeking innovative R&D solutions.

Teaming & Collaboration

AetherVision LLC actively seeks strategic partnerships to enhance capabilities and deliver comprehensive solutions. We welcome collaboration with:

  • Prime Contractors: Serving as a dedicated, agile, and innovative subcontractor on major programs, bringing specialized expertise in AI/ML, neuromorphic sensing, and advanced ISR.
  • Other Small Businesses (including SDVOSBs, VOSBs, WOSBs): Teaming to pursue joint contract opportunities and leverage complementary skills.
  • University Research Groups & FFRDCs: Collaborating on fundamental research, technology transition, and leveraging cutting-edge academic advancements.

Our goal is to build synergistic relationships that accelerate the delivery of impactful technologies to the warfighter and intelligence analyst.
