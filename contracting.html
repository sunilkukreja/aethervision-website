Government Contracting & Partnering

Verified SDVOSB Partner for Mission-Critical Solutions

AetherVision LLC is a formally verified Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), deeply committed to addressing the critical technology requirements of the U.S. Government, particularly the Department of Defense (DoD) and the Intelligence Community (IC). Our verified SDVOSB status provides government agencies with streamlined acquisition pathways, including dedicated set-aside contracts and, under specific circumstances, sole-source acquisitions as permitted by the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR).

We are registered and active in the System for Award Management (SAM.gov). We actively pursue opportunities where our distinct capabilities provide significant value and potential justification for sole-source consideration under FAR Part 19.1406. This may apply when an agency's urgent or highly specialized requirements are uniquely met by AetherVision's specific technical approach, proprietary innovations (e.g., stemming from our neuromorphic R&D), or the unparalleled expertise demonstrated through successful execution of high-risk programs like the DARPA FENCE TRL-6 prototype delivery.

Commitment to Uncompromising Compliance & Security

AetherVision understands that operating within the national security ecosystem demands rigorous adherence to security protocols and compliance mandates. We are architecting our operations, infrastructure, and processes with a steadfast commitment to implementing and maintaining robust cybersecurity practices aligned with critical government standards.

  • NIST SP 800-171 & DFARS 252.204-7012: We are committed to meeting the requirements for protecting Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) as outlined in NIST SP 800-171 and mandated by DFARS Clause 252.204-7012.
  • CMMC Readiness: We are actively preparing our systems and policies to meet the appropriate Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) level as dictated by future contract requirements, leveraging founder leadership experience in establishing secure government cloud environments (e.g., AWS GovCloud, Azure Government) and adhering to DoD Risk Management Framework (RMF) principles.
  • ITAR/EAR Compliance: Our team maintains diligent awareness of and strict adherence to export control regulations, including the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) and Export Administration Regulations (EAR), pertinent to defense-related technologies and technical data.

Protecting sensitive government information, intellectual property, and mission data integrity is paramount throughout our entire research, development, testing, and deployment lifecycle.

Contracting Vehicles & Strategic Focus

AetherVision LLC is positioned to engage with government clients and partners through a variety of established contracting mechanisms, leveraging both our SDVOSB status and deep technical strengths:

  • SBIR/STTR Programs: Actively pursuing Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) and Small Business Technology Transfer (STTR) Phase I, Phase II, Direct to Phase II (DP2), and Phase III opportunities across relevant DoD components (e.g., DARPA, AFRL, ARL, ONR, SOCOM, Army Futures Command, NAVAIR, NAVSEA, DIU) and Intelligence Community agencies (e.g., IARPA, NRO, NGA) where our core competencies align with topic requirements.
  • SDVOSB Set-Aside Contracts: Competitively targeting specific SDVOSB set-aside solicitations published on platforms like SAM.gov that directly match our specialized capabilities in AI/ML, Neuromorphic Vision, Advanced Sensing, ISR, and demonstrated performance indicators (including DARPA FENCE program success).
  • Sole-Source Opportunities (FAR 19.1406): Engaging proactively with agency Program Managers and Contracting Officers where our unique technological differentiators (e.g., proprietary neuromorphic approaches, novel AI algorithms) or the highly specialized expertise of our leadership directly and uniquely meet an agency's specific requirements, providing potential justification for a non-competitive sole-source award under the SDVOSB program.
  • Subcontracting & Teaming: Actively seeking strategic partnerships with established prime contractors (including large defense contractors and mid-tier system integrators) and complementary small businesses to contribute niche expertise, innovative technology components, and SDVOSB participation credit to larger, complex programs.
  • Broad Agency Announcements (BAAs) & Other Transactions (OTs): Proactively monitoring and responding to relevant BAAs seeking innovative R&D solutions in our areas of expertise. Exploring Other Transaction Agreements (OTAs) for rapid prototyping and fielding opportunities that align with our agile development approach.

Teaming & Collaboration: Building Synergistic Partnerships

AetherVision LLC believes in the power of collaboration to accelerate innovation and deliver comprehensive, mission-focused solutions. We actively seek strategic collaborations and are eager to partner with:

  • Prime Contractors: Serving as a dedicated, agile, and innovative SDVOSB subcontractor on major defense and intelligence programs. We offer unique, niche expertise in areas such as neuromorphic sensor design and integration, edge AI/ML algorithm optimization for low-SWaP platforms, advanced multi-INT sensor fusion techniques, secure system architecture development, and rapid prototyping.
  • Other Small Businesses: Forming joint ventures or prime/sub relationships with fellow small businesses (including other SDVOSBs, VOSBs, WOSBs, SDBs, HubZone firms) to pursue specific contract opportunities, leveraging complementary technical skills, combined socioeconomic advantages, and a shared commitment to mission success.
  • University Research Centers & FFRDCs: Collaborating on fundamental research challenges, facilitating efficient technology transition (T2) pathways from laboratory discovery to fielded capability, and leveraging cutting-edge academic advancements relevant to pressing national security needs.

Our objective is to forge synergistic, trust-based relationships that accelerate the development, maturation, and fielding of impactful, next-generation technologies benefiting the warfighter, intelligence analyst, and homeland security professional.
