Government Contracting & Partnering

SDVOSB Certified Partner for Mission-Critical Solutions

AetherVision LLC is a formally verified Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), deeply committed to addressing the critical mission requirements of the U.S. Government, particularly the Department of Defense and the Intelligence Community. Our SDVOSB status enables government agencies to partner with us efficiently through dedicated set-aside contracts and potentially through sole-source acquisitions, facilitating rapid access to our unique technological innovations and specialized expertise.

We are registered in the System for Award Management (SAM.gov) and are actively pursuing opportunities where our distinct capabilities provide significant value. Our unique qualifications—including pioneering work in neuromorphic sensing exemplified by the successful execution and TRL-6 prototype delivery for the **DARPA FENCE program**, coupled with the founder's specific multi-domain leadership experience across defense R&D (Raytheon, NASA) and advanced AI/ML applications—may provide justification for sole-source considerations under FAR Part 19.1406 when specific, urgent, or highly specialized requirements necessitate our unique technical approach or expertise.

Commitment to Uncompromising Compliance & Security

AetherVision understands that operating within the national security domain demands rigorous adherence to security and compliance protocols. We are architecting our operations and systems with a steadfast commitment to implementing robust cybersecurity practices aligned with essential standards, including NIST SP 800-171 and DFARS 252.204-7012 requirements. We are actively preparing to meet the appropriate Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) level as dictated by specific contract requirements, leveraging prior leadership experience in establishing secure cloud environments (e.g., AWS GovCloud) and adhering to DoD Risk Management Framework (RMF) principles.

Our team maintains diligent awareness of and adherence to export control regulations (ITAR/EAR) pertinent to defense-related technologies. Protecting sensitive information, intellectual property, and mission data integrity is paramount throughout our entire research, development, and deployment lifecycle.

Contracting Vehicles & Strategic Focus

AetherVision LLC is positioned to leverage its SDVOSB status and deep technical strengths through a variety of contracting mechanisms:

  • SBIR/STTR Programs: Actively pursuing Phase I, Phase II, and Phase III opportunities across relevant DoD components (e.g., DARPA, AFRL, ARL, ONR, SOCOM, Army Futures Command, NAVAIR, NAVSEA) and Intelligence Community agencies (e.g., IARPA, NRO, NGA) where our core competencies in AI/ML, Neuromorphic Vision, Advanced Sensing, and ISR align with program needs.
  • SDVOSB Set-Aside Contracts: Targeting specific SDVOSB set-aside solicitations that directly match our specialized capabilities and demonstrated past performance indicators (including DARPA program success).
  • Sole-Source Opportunities: Engaging with agencies where our unique technological differentiators (e.g., proprietary neuromorphic approaches, novel AI algorithms) or highly specialized expertise directly meet an agency's specific requirements, potentially justifying a sole-source award under the SDVOSB program.
  • Subcontracting & Teaming: Seeking partnerships with established prime contractors (e.g., Raytheon, Lockheed Martin, Northrop Grumman, BAE Systems, L3Harris) and complementary small businesses to contribute specialized expertise to larger, complex programs.
  • Broad Agency Announcements (BAAs) & Other Transactions (OTs): Proactively monitoring and responding to relevant BAAs and exploring OT agreements that seek innovative R&D solutions in our areas of expertise, such as advanced sensing, AI/ML for ISR, and autonomous systems.

Teaming & Collaboration: Building Synergistic Partnerships

AetherVision LLC actively seeks strategic collaborations to amplify capabilities and deliver comprehensive, mission-focused solutions. We are eager to partner with:

  • Prime Contractors: Serving as a dedicated, agile, and innovative SDVOSB subcontractor on major defense and intelligence programs, contributing unique, niche expertise in areas like neuromorphic sensor design and integration, edge AI/ML algorithm optimization for low-SWaP platforms, advanced multi-INT sensor fusion, and secure system architecture development.
  • Other Small Businesses (including SDVOSBs, VOSBs, WOSBs, HubZone): Forming joint ventures or prime/sub relationships to pursue specific contract opportunities, leveraging complementary technical skills, socioeconomic advantages, and shared mission focus.
  • University Research Centers & Federally Funded Research and Development Centers (FFRDCs): Collaborating on fundamental research challenges, facilitating efficient technology transition (T2) from lab to field, and leveraging cutting-edge academic advancements relevant to pressing national security needs.

Our objective is to forge synergistic relationships that accelerate the development and fielding of impactful, next-generation technologies for the warfighter, intelligence analyst, and homeland security professional.
