Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To develop and deliver groundbreaking sensing, AI, and autonomous systems technologies that enhance national security, amplify intelligence capabilities, and provide decisive overmatch against evolving global threats.

Vision: To be the premier SDVOSB technology partner for the Intelligence Community and DoD, recognized for delivering transformative AI/ML, ISR, and neuromorphic solutions rooted in deep technical expertise and an unwavering commitment to U.S. national security.

Core Capabilities

AetherVision translates cutting-edge research into mission-ready capabilities, specializing in:

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) & Machine Learning (ML): Developing advanced algorithms for real-time multi-INT data analysis, threat detection, pattern recognition, predictive modeling, and computer vision, optimized for deployment from cloud to edge devices in contested environments.
  • Intelligence, Surveillance & Reconnaissance (ISR) Solutions: Designing and integrating advanced sensor payloads and processing for ISR platforms (including UAVs/UGVs). Expertise includes multi-sensor fusion (EO/IR, radar, SIGINT), edge computing, autonomous navigation algorithms, and secure data links for comprehensive situational awareness.
  • Neuromorphic Vision Systems: Pioneering next-generation event-driven visual perception. Expertise includes custom ROIC/FPA design and leveraging neuromorphic principles for high-speed, high-dynamic-range, low-SWaP sensing in challenging conditions (e.g., low light, high clutter, fast-moving targets).
  • Advanced Data Analysis & Exploitation: Creating novel methods for multi-INT fusion (SIGINT, GEOINT, MASINT, HUMINT, OSINT), predictive analytics, anomaly detection, and automated intelligence workflows to extract actionable intelligence from complex, large-scale datasets.
  • Expert Consulting & Strategic Technology Planning: Providing specialized consulting in AI/ML, ISR system design, neuromorphic sensing applications, multi-INT integration, and technology roadmapping tailored to specific agency needs and mission requirements.

About AetherVision: Proven Leadership & Innovation

AetherVision LLC is a 100% Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) founded and led by Dr. Sunil Kukreja, a service-disabled veteran (U.S. Air Force & Army) with over 20 years of executive leadership experience driving high-impact R&D across defense, aerospace, and federal sectors (including NASA and Raytheon Technologies Research Center). Dr. Kukreja possesses a proven track record of successfully leading complex, mission-critical programs, including the high-impact DARPA FENCE program ($5.5M, TRL-6 prototype delivery exceeding milestones).

Based in Connecticut and registered in Delaware, AetherVision combines deep technical expertise in AI/ML, neuromorphic engineering, autonomous systems, and sensor fusion with an agile, mission-focused approach to deliver beyond-next-generation solutions addressing the nation's most critical security challenges.
