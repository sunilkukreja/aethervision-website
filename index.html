About AetherVision: Proven Leadership & Innovation

AetherVision LLC is a 100% Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), formally verified and registered in SAM.gov. Founded and led by Dr. Sunil Kukreja, a service-disabled veteran (U.S. Air Force & Army), the company leverages over 20 years of his executive leadership experience driving high-impact R&D across defense (Raytheon Technologies Research Center), aerospace (NASA), and international academia (NUS).

Dr. Kukreja's proven track record includes successfully leading complex, mission-critical programs, notably conceptualizing, pitching, and directing the $5.5M DARPA FENCE program culminating in a successful TRL-6 prototype delivery exceeding all milestones. His prior leadership experience managing large R&D portfolios ($60M+) and spearheading initiatives in secure cloud infrastructure (AWS/Azure Gov) and advanced AI/ML platform development within major defense contractor environments informs AetherVision's approach.

Operating as a Connecticut Limited Liability Company based in Glastonbury, CT, AetherVision combines deep technical expertise in AI/ML, neuromorphic engineering, autonomous systems, and sensor fusion with an agile, mission-focused methodology to deliver beyond-next-generation solutions addressing the nation's most critical security challenges.
