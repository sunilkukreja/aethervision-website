A Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) delivering disruptive AI/ML, Neuromorphic Vision, and ISR technologies for the U.S. Intelligence Community and Department of Defense.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To develop and deliver groundbreaking technologies that enhance national security, improve intelligence capabilities, and provide a decisive advantage in an ever-evolving threat landscape.

Vision: To be the premier provider of advanced AI/ML, ISR, and neuromorphic vision technologies to the IC and DoD, recognized for disruptive innovations and unwavering commitment to national security.

Core Capabilities

AetherVision specializes in the research, development, and application of cutting-edge technologies:

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) & Machine Learning (ML): Advanced algorithms for real-time analysis, threat detection, pattern recognition, predictive modeling, NLP, and computer vision, optimized for edge devices.
  • Intelligence, Surveillance & Reconnaissance (ISR) Platforms: Advanced UAV/UGV platforms integrating multi-sensor systems (EO/IR, radar, SIGINT), edge computing, autonomous navigation, and secure communications.
  • Neuromorphic Vision Systems: Next-generation event-driven cameras and sensors leveraging custom ROICs/FPAs for high-speed, high-dynamic-range, low-power visual perception in challenging environments.
  • Data Analysis & Exploitation: Novel methods for multi-INT fusion, predictive analytics, anomaly detection, and data visualization to extract actionable intelligence from complex data.
  • Consulting & Training: Expert services in AI/ML, ISR, data analysis, neuromorphic vision, system design, and strategic technology planning.

About AetherVision

Founded by Dr. Sunil Kukreja, a service-disabled veteran with extensive leadership experience across high-impact roles at Raytheon Technologies Research Center, NASA, the National University of Singapore, and within DARPA programs, AetherVision LLC is uniquely positioned to tackle complex national security challenges. As a 100% SDVOSB based in Connecticut and registered in Delaware, we leverage unique expertise, a proven track record in delivering transformative technologies, and an agile approach to deliver beyond-next-generation solutions.
