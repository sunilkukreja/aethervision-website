About AetherVision LLC

Our Company

AetherVision LLC, founded by Dr. Sunil Kukreja, is a Delaware-based, 100% service-disabled veteran-owned small business (SDVOSB) dedicated to developing and delivering disruptive technologies for the Intelligence Community (IC) and the Department of Defense (DoD). We specialize in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI/ML), Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance (ISR), neuromorphic vision, and multi-spectral defense solutions designed to address critical national security challenges.

As an SDVOSB, we are eligible for preferential treatment in government contracting, allowing us to effectively pursue opportunities to serve the nation. Our initial operations are based in Avon, CT, maintaining agility and low overhead as we grow.

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission: To develop and deliver groundbreaking technologies that enhance national security, improve intelligence capabilities, and provide a decisive advantage in an ever-evolving threat landscape.

Vision: To be the premier provider of advanced AI/ML, ISR, and neuromorphic vision technologies to the IC and DoD, recognized for its disruptive innovations, unwavering commitment to national security, and exceptional service to its clients.

Our Core Values:

  • Patriotism: Dedicated to serving the United States and protecting its interests.
  • Integrity: Maintaining unwavering ethical conduct and transparency.
  • Innovation: Relentlessly pursuing groundbreaking technologies.
  • Excellence: Delivering exceptional results and exceeding expectations.
  • Collaboration: Fostering partnerships to achieve shared goals.
  • Agility: Adapting quickly to changing needs and advancements.
  • Inclusion: Committing to a diverse workplace, prioritizing veterans and individuals with disabilities.

Our Founder: Dr. Sunil Kukreja

AetherVision is led by Dr. Sunil Kukreja, a visionary senior executive and service-disabled veteran (U.S. Air Force & Army) with over 20 years of experience leading high-impact R&D in defense, aerospace, and federal sectors. With a Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering from McGill University and significant leadership roles at Raytheon Technologies Research Center (RTRC), NASA, and the National University of Singapore (NUS), Dr. Kukreja brings a unique blend of deep technical proficiency and strategic leadership.

His expertise spans AI/ML, robotics, autonomous systems, neuromorphic engineering, sensor fusion, systems engineering (MBSE), and cybersecurity. At RTRC, Dr. Kukreja spearheaded critical initiatives, including leading the successful execution of the high-impact DARPA FENCE program ($5.5M funding, TRL-6 prototype delivery exceeding milestones). His leadership extended to architecting secure cloud systems (AWS GovCloud) for multi-INT ISR data analysis, directing AI-powered robotic inspection systems achieving significant cost savings and safety improvements, and overseeing complex DoD program portfolios involving AI/ML platforms for autonomous systems. This included managing large multidisciplinary teams, implementing robust technical oversight adhering to ITAR and cybersecurity standards (NIST/RMF), driving Agile transformations, adopting Infrastructure as Code (IaC) and cloud automation across major platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP), and ensuring rigorous risk management (FMEA) and safety assurance for high-consequence operations.

Prior to his industry leadership, Dr. Kukreja made significant contributions at NASA, leading teams developing critical AI/ML models for flight safety (F/A-18 flutter prediction) and astronaut health monitoring, establishing gold-standard data governance frameworks, and championing MBSE adoption. At NUS, he built and directed a world-renowned Neuromorphic Engineering & Robotics research center, securing substantial funding ($10M+) and leading advancements in areas like neuromorphic tactile sensing and cognitive load analysis for high-stress environments.

Dr. Kukreja's career demonstrates a consistent ability to manage complex technical programs, lead large teams, secure funding, deliver cutting-edge technology, and foster cultures of technical excellence, safety, and inclusion with a strong commitment to mentoring and veteran hiring.
