About AetherVision LLC

Pioneering Advanced Technology for National Security

AetherVision LLC is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) founded with the mission to develop and deliver disruptive technological solutions addressing critical challenges faced by the Department of Defense (DoD), Intelligence Community (IC), and other federal agencies. We specialize in the research, development, and application of cutting-edge technologies at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Neuromorphic Engineering, and advanced ISR (Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance) platforms.

Our foundation rests upon deep technical expertise and a proven history of translating complex scientific principles into tangible, high-impact capabilities. We are driven by a commitment to innovation, agility, and unwavering dedication to supporting the nation's security objectives.

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission: To empower the U.S. Government with decisive technological advantages by developing and deploying next-generation sensing, perception, and intelligence solutions.

Vision: To be the leading SDVOSB innovator, recognized for pioneering transformative technologies that redefine capabilities in national security and intelligence domains.

Core Values:

  • Innovation: Relentlessly pursuing novel approaches and breakthrough technologies.
  • Integrity: Upholding the highest ethical standards in all operations and interactions.
  • Excellence: Striving for superior quality and impactful results in every endeavor.
  • Agility: Responding rapidly and effectively to evolving customer needs and mission requirements.
  • Collaboration: Building strong partnerships to achieve shared objectives and enhance collective capabilities.
  • Commitment: Dedication to the mission success of our clients and the well-being of our nation and its veterans.

Founder's Expertise: Dr. Sunil Kukreja, Ph.D.

AetherVision was founded by Dr. Sunil Kukreja, a U.S. Veteran (Air Force & Army, Honorable Discharge) whose career embodies a unique convergence of military service, advanced academic research, and high-impact technology development within the defense and aerospace sectors. His experience spans critical roles at NASA, Raytheon Technologies Research Center, and the National University of Singapore (NUS), providing him with unparalleled multi-domain expertise.

At NASA, Dr. Kukreja served as a Senior Principal Research Scientist, leading research programs focused on astronaut health, advanced control systems for high-performance aircraft (like the F/A-18), and pioneering AI/ML applications for predictive modeling and system analysis, contributing significantly to mission safety and effectiveness.

During his tenure as Associate Director of AI, Machine Learning, and Robotics at Raytheon Technologies Research Center, Dr. Kukreja conceptualized, pitched, led, and executed critical programs for DARPA and other DoD agencies. This included leadership of the highly successful $5.5M FENCE program (Fast Event-driven Neuromorphic Camera for Enhanced situational awareness), which exceeded all milestones and delivered a TRL-6 prototype demonstrating revolutionary neuromorphic vision capabilities for threat detection with significant improvements in sensitivity, false alarm reduction, and power efficiency. His work consistently involved developing secure, AI-powered systems for ISR, autonomous robotics, predictive maintenance, and multi-swarm autonomy, often managing large, geographically dispersed teams and complex budgets.

As an Associate Professor and Director of Neuromorphic Engineering & Robotics at NUS, Dr. Kukreja built and led a world-renowned research team, securing $10 million in competitive funding. His research drove significant advancements in surgical robotics (achieving a 40% increase in precision for a novel platform), AI-powered medical devices, neuromorphic sensing, and human performance analysis, resulting in numerous high-impact publications and patents.

Dr. Kukreja's deep technical expertise encompasses AI/ML, neuromorphic computing, event-based sensing, robotics, ISR platforms, sensor fusion, advanced algorithms, secure cloud architectures, and edge computing. His proven ability to secure funding, lead complex programs (up to $60M), drive innovation from concept to deployment, and navigate demanding technical and regulatory landscapes forms the bedrock of AetherVision's capabilities. His commitment extends to fostering diversity and mentoring the next generation of engineers and scientists.

Our Commitment

As an SDVOSB founded and led by a veteran, AetherVision is deeply committed to supporting the warfighter and the intelligence professional. We prioritize hiring and mentoring fellow veterans, recognizing the invaluable skills, dedication, and mission focus they bring. We strive to build a diverse and inclusive team dedicated to excellence and delivering solutions that make a tangible difference in safeguarding national security.
